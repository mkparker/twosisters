About Us

Two Sisters U-Pick Apricots

Two Sisters U-Pick Apricots was the vision of two sisters who happened to have medical issues at the same exact time. These issues forced both sisters to stay home while recovering, and they both got to stare out the window day after day and watch the apricots go from blossoms to fruit. So, as the medical bills started to arrive, they came up with the idea to do a U-Pick farm after years of selling their crops commercially. This will give them the opportunity to interact with lots of people, after being stuck inside for weeks on end, and do something fun as sisters.
